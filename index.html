Music

I've been a classically trained violinist for almost my entire life. I like to play in ensembles like the Civic Orchestra of Victoria (I am currently the principal second violin there) and the Sooke Philharmonic. I've also played in the Lake Union Civic Orchestra, the Octava Chamber Orchestra, and the Seattle Collaborative Orchestra, as well as in musical theater pits, in chamber ensembles, by myself, and anywhere else I can.

I write about my upcoming performances in my newsletter, so you can sign up and hear about them about a week in advance. I send about one per month.

Other Stuff

I take pictures and make drawings! They mostly end up on Flickr.

Bike touring is my favorite way to travel. Drop me a line and ask me about routes, especially in the Pacific Northwest. You can also ask me about cargo biking or bike camping.

Playing music is fun, but listening to music is also fun. I like literally every kind of music - if it reminds you of listening to a raccoon that fell into a trash bin, I might enjoy it.

I have been doing Chen style tai chi for over a decade now, and I've started assistant teaching in some of my partner's classes.
